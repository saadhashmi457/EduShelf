About EduShelf

A modern book exchange, sale, and donation platform built for students and readers to share knowledge efficiently and freely.

Who We Are

EduShelf is your go-to digital platform designed to connect book owners with readers across Pakistan Educational Institutes. Whether you want to sell, donate, or exchange — our community is here for you.

We empower education through accessibility, affordability, and collaboration. Our platform streamlines the process of book sharing using modern design and smooth user experience.

Student-Centric

Built for students by students. We understand the struggle of access and cost — and we’re here to change that.

Accessible Book Platform

Whether it’s academic, fiction, or professional — we help you discover and share books quickly and freely.

Driven by Impact

Our mission is to foster a giving culture while promoting reading and educational growth.

What we offer

Exchange Books

Post your books and find someone to swap with. Easy, quick, and hassle-free exchange between users.

Sell Books

List your books for sale and let buyers find you by category, condition, and availability.

Free Donations

Give books away to those in need and help spread education and positivity.

How it Works

1. Sign Up

Create a free account to start exchanging books.

2. Post Book

List your book with images, condition, and category.

3. See My-Listings

Check My Listing Page to manage your books.

4. My-Requests

User Who make request for book can check all Requests.

5. Connect

Receive requests after admin approval and chat with Uploader users.

6. Exchange

Complete the book handover securely and smoothly.
