How do I sign up for EduShelf?

To sign up, visit the Signup page and enter your full name, father name, department, university email, roll number, and password. After registration, login with your university email and password.

Can I exchange books with other students?

Yes! EduShelf lets you sell, exchange, or donate books. Browse listings, contact sellers, and arrange exchanges easily.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. EduShelf uses secure protocols and never shares your details without your consent. Please avoid sharing sensitive info publicly.

How can I reset my password?

Click the “Forgot password?” link on the login page to get an email with instructions to reset your password.

Can I list books for free?

Yes, you can list books as free, for sale, or exchange by choosing the category when uploading your listing.
